About

Gregory Casparian is a New York based Projection and Lighting Designer. He has designed for the New York Deaf Theatre, and assisted on shows with Tectonic, Atlantic, and Ensemble for the Romantic Century. Outside New York, he has designed for theater, dance, opera, corporate events, rock & roll, and aerial performance.

Gregory is a fan of storytelling of all types, whether on stage, page, screen, or around a campfire.
